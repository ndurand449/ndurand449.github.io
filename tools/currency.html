Currency Forecast: EUR/USD

Year-to-date actual data

Actual exchange rates are retrieved monthly from APILayer. Output shows the average, minimum and maximum rates for the selected year, and the dates these occurred.

Forecast average data

Analysts' forecasts are entered monthly for different time periods over the next 12 months. Combined with actual data year-to-date, these forecasts are used to calculate a range of average rates for the selected year. Output shows the range of averages, one calculated with the average forecasts, a second calculated with the lower forecasts, and a third calculated with the upper forecasts.

Forecast graph with historical rates

The forecast data is shown in a Google Chart graph. The blue line on the left shows the actual year-to-date data for the prior year and the selected year. The colored lines on the right show the forecast range for the remainder of the selected year.
